About Us

"Tiongco's Sisters Restaurant sincerely thanks you for visiting and supporting us. Our goal is to give you a genuine experience of Chinese cuisine, using fresh ingredients from local farmers. We have worked hard to revive traditional cooking methods to provide delicious and helpful food. We focused on delivering excellent service in a great dining experience, a long with favorite dishes like Kung Pao Chicken, Beeef Chow Mein and Sweet and Sour Pork. Thank you again for your continued support!"

"Growing up in China we were surrounded by the sights, smells, and taste of traditional Chinese food. Our love for food and culture led us to create a menu that showcases the best flavors and traditions of China. We aim to bring back ancient cooking methods and provide a rich experience for every visitor."

At Tiongco's Sisters Restaurant, we use fresh products from local farmers to prepare authentic Chinese dishes. We believe that excellent service and delicious food are the keys to an unforgettable dining experience. Thats why we sincerely thank you for patronizing and supporting our bussiness.
